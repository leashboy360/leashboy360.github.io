Why Satellite Doge-1 ?

The Doge-1 mission aims to send a CubeSat satellite, funded by Dogecoin, to the moon. This mission gained attention when Elon Musk tweeted about it. It's set for a launch in Q1 of 2024, specifically 12 January 2024.

Geometric Energy runs the mission, using a SpaceX rocket for transport. Intuitive Machines, managing the moon ride share, incorporates Doge-1 into a bigger mission with their IM-1 lander. The schedule is subject to the usual industry adjustments based on ongoing preparations and conditions.
